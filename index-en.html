Delicious comfort food & drinks, authentic flavours and great atmosphere

Los Gorditos is a casual dining Tex-Mex & Mexican restaurant in Kazimierz Krakow. We serve Mexican & Tex-Mex comfort food for dine-in, delivery and pick-up. Our specialty dishes include burritos, burrito bowls, tacos, quesadillas, nachos and freshly-made tortilla chips with salsas.

All our food is made from fresh ingredients we make in-house. We also serve delicious margaritas, beers, lemonades, and many more tasty alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.
